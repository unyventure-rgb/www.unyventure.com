About U&Y Venture

A service business built on long-term relationships and dependable delivery.

U&Y Venture is based in Melbourne, Australia and operates under two entities: U&Y Venture in Australia and U&Y Venture Sdn. Bhd. in Malaysia. Originating in Melbourne in 2012, the business has grown to deliver a broad range of products and services across multiple sectors.

Our approach is simple: understand the client, tailor the solution and deliver with consistency. We work to provide practical outcomes with a focus on quality, efficiency, sustainability and professional care.
