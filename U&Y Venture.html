About Us

U&Y Venture is based in Melbourne, Australia and we operate under two entities (U&Y Venture, Australia) and (U&Y Venture Sdn. Bhd, Malaysia). We have provided various products & services across Australia for more than 12 years. Originated in Melbourne, Australia in 2012 and have a strong mark, U&Y Venture & U&Y Venture Sdn Bhd offers a robust service delivery model working with each organisation and client to deliver a customised and cost-efficient products & services with a focus on people, sustainability and best practice in the industry. 

U&Y Venture & U&Y Venture Sdn Bhd proudly collaborate with a diverse range of clients with over 100 dedicated employees in Malaysia and a network of 70 subcontractor teams in Australia, we are committed to delivering exceptional service and results across all projects. across multiple industry sectors which include long term relationships such as  School ,Medical Centre ,Childcare Centre ,Pilates ,Offices, Hostel Cleaning and Real Estate Clientele.
